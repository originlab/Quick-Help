Worksheets

The worksheet's primary purpose is to organize your raw data and your analysis results, but it also serves important data manipulation, exploration, and plotting functions. The worksheet looks much like a spreadsheet, but it differs in some important ways.

Worksheet naming conventions have been covered in the section entitled Worksheet Column and Cell Range Naming Conventions.
