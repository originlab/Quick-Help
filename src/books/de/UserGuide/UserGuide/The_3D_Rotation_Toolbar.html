The 3D Rotation Toolbar

3D-Rotation-Toolbar

The 3D Rotation toolbar is available when a 3D graph is active (worksheet- or matrix-derived). Toolbar buttons rotate the graph, change the perspective and reset to the default settings.

You can also rotate, resize and stretch a 3D graph by dragging the mouse.

Rotate the graph on any direction.

Rotate counterclockwise on the Z axis by the specified rotation angle. Note: For 3D XYY graphs, rotate counterclockwise on the Y axis; for 3D ternary graphs, rotate counterclockwise on the Zh axis.

Rotate clockwise on the Z axis by the specified rotation angle. Note: For 3D XYY graphs, rotate clockwise on the Y axis; for 3D ternary graphs, rotate clockwise on the Zh axis.

Tilt the top of the graph counterclockwise the specified rotation angle.

Tilt the top of the graph clockwise the specified rotation angle.

Rotate downward on the X axis the specified rotation angle.

Rotate upward on the X axis the specified rotation angle.

Increase the perspective angle by 3 degrees.

Decrease the perspective angle by 3 degrees.

Fit the graph to the layer frame.

Reset the cube angle and perspective angle only.

Reset the cube rotation, axis length, shear and projection to the default settings.

Select or type a rotation angle (in degrees) for the rotation buttons.
