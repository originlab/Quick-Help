FAQ-943 Is there a way to make simultaneous edits to all panels in a multi-panel graph?

Simultaneous-edits-multi-panel-graph

Last Update: 6/8/2018

Beginning with Origin 2018b, you can make edits to one panel in a multi-panel graph and have your edits apply to other panels in the graph. This behavior is turned on for most multi-panel graph templates, by default.

Plot Details Common Display Controls

This behavior is controlled by the Common Display group settings on the Layers tab of Plot Details. Once controls are configured, you can make simultaneous edits using Plot Details controls or the appropriate Style and Format toolbar buttons.

For information, see The (Plot Details) Layers Tab controls.
