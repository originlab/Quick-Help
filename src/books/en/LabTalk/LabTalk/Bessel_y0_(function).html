3.5.1.3.19 Bessel_y0

Bessel-y0-func

Definition:

evaluates the Bessel function of the second kind, , x > 0. The approximation is based on Chebyshev expansions.

For more information please review the s17acc function in the NAG document.


Parameters:

x (input, double)
The argument x of the function.
Constraint: x>0.0
(output, double)
The approximation of the Bessel function.
