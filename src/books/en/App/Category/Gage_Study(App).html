2.19 Gage Study (Pro)

GG

Gage Study is a methodology used in quality management and statistical analysis to assess the measurement system (or gauge) used to collect data in a manufacturing or business process. The purpose of a Gage Study is to evaluate the reliability, accuracy, and precision of the measurement system itself, separate from the variation of the actual process being measured.

The Gage Study - Continuous Data app provide following tools:

  • Type 1 Gage Study
  • Gage Linear Bias Analysis
  • Crossed Gage R&R Study
  • Nested Gage R&R Study
  • Expanded Gage R&R Study
  • Attribute Gage Study
  • Create Gage Worksheet

How to download and install the app
