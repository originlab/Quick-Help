Introduction

Capability analysis can be used to assess whether a process able to produce products that meet the specifications by providing following quality tools. It is used to

  • Determine whether the process meets the specifications
  • How will the process perform in the future
  • Provide insight into how to improve the process and sustain your improvements.

Processing

Select a capability analysis basing on your data
