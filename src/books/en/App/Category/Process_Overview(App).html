Introduction

The Process Overview generates a combination report with charts below. It enables you to assess the quality or capability of a process in a glance

Xbar / Individuals charts Access whether the process is stable
R / S / MR chart Monitor the process variation and assesses whether the process variation is in control
Run chart Study the data trends or pattern
Probability plot Verify that a chosen distribution fits your data.
Capability histogram Visually compare the distribution of data from your process to the specification spread
Capability plot
Capability statistics Assess capability of your process quantitatively.

Processing
