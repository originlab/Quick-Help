Panning to Major/Minor Ticks

(ORG-8511)

When panning the axis in a graph, three modes are now supported:

  • Pan by percent: Default behavior. Every time +/-/Left/Right key is pressed using mouse wheel to scroll once will pan by percent.
  • Pan by major ticks: Every time +/-/Left/Right key is pressed using mouse wheel to scroll once will pan to next/previous major ticks.
  • Pan by minor ticks: Every time +/-/Left/Right key is pressed using mouse wheel to scroll once will pan to next/previous minor ticks.
