Fill Area Between Two Line Plots

Sample OPJ download to try: Fill Area between Curves.opj 

(ORG-3693) (ORG-9716)

 

  • Filling area is between the curve and the one below it in the Layer Contents dialog, try modify the sequence of plots to get your desired graph.

How to:

  1. Go to Plot Details: Plot level: Line tab, check Fill Area Under Curve checkbox.
  2. In dragdown list, select Fill to next data plot - One Color or Fill to next data plot - Above Below Colors.
  3. In new appeared Pattern tab, set the filling details.
