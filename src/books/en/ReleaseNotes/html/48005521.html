ORG-11510Mean symbol failed to show in in Box Chart Legend in 9.1 SR3

Right click legend in box chart and choose Box Chart Components. Check Mean to show Mean symbol in legend. It shows as an empty box in 9.1 SR3.

No such problem in 9.1 SR2. So if you need this feature, please reinstall 9.1 SR2. If you don't have the Setup files, contact Tech@originlab.com for it.
