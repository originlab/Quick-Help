6.79 FAQ-1091 My Learning Edition is expiring. How can I renew it?

LE-Expire-Renew

Last Update: 9/12/2021

A reminder message dialog will pop up 21 days before the Learning Edition license expires.

If you see this message, you can follow steps below to renew the Learning Edition:

  1. Open web browser to https://www.originlab.com/index.aspx?go=PRODUCTS/OriginStudentVersion.
  2. Click Request Learning Edition button.
  3. Fill out the form as required and click Submit button to apply for a license renewal.
  4. A survey on Origin features and feedbacks will be sent to you. Please complete the survey.
  5. If your renewal request has been approved, OriginLab will email you with the renewal instruction. Follow the instruction to renew your Learning Edition.
