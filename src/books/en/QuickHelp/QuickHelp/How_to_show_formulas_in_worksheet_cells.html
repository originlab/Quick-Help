2.113 FAQ-1042 How to show formulas in worksheet cells?

Show-Cell-Formula

Last Update: 3/16/2020


When you enter a formula in a worksheet cell, the formula will be calculated and the result value is displayed. In the prevous versions, you must enter the in-place edit mode - double click inside the cell to see the formula. From Origin 2020b, you can click upper left corner of worksheet and click Show Formula button on the mini toolbar that appears.

Keywords:cell formula, show formula
