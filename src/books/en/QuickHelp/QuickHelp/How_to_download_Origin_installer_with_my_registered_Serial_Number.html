6.85 FAQ-1103 How to download Origin installer with my registered Serial Number?

Download-Installer

Last Update: 5/16/2023

To download the Origin installer of current version:

  1. Log in OriginLab website with your web account.
  2. On the Welcome page, click “Origin Current Version Installer” link under Downloads section.
|

To download the Origin installer of a registered version (highest eligible/not current version):

  1. Log in OriginLab website with your web account.
  2. On the Welcome page, click “Manage My License” link.
    |
  3. This page will list all serial numbers you registered and the corresponding highest versions. To download the installer of a serial number, click that serial number link.
    |
  4. On the Origin Product Registration | License request history webpage, click Download this version button to get the highest version (of latest Service Release) of this serial number
    |
