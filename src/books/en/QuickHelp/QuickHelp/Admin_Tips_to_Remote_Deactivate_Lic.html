6.65 FAQ-903 I am the administrator of a large organization, when my users have to request to use Origin. It is hard to know who are using Origin. Is there a way to release/recover the license from some users?

Admin-Tips-to-Remote-deactivate-Lic

Last Update: 11/12/2017

As a benefit of maintenance service, you can use “remote deactivate” to release the product key/license immediately. The old Origin will be deactivated and new member can use the product key to obtain a license immediately. Note: This requires Origin to have internet access for checking validity of the license.

Refer to the second tip in this page -> "If you are the administrator of the package" section for detailed steps of remote deactivating a product key.
