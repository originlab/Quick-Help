4.53 FAQ-712 Why do my LabTalk scripts from version 7.x fail in version 8.x or 9.x?

Old-lt-fails

Last Update: 7/25/2018

Several significant improvements were made to LabTalk scripting in version 8.0, mainly to support multiple-sheet workbooks. These changes may cause some compatibility issues with older script, but these issues are typically easy to fix. Please refer to this page to learn how to modify your scripts to be compatible with new versions.

In addition, you can refer to the Labtalk document for the most up-to-date information for Labtalk programming.
