Data Requirements

  • Matrix: A matrix sheet. Multi-object sheet is supported.

or

  • Worksheet: worksheet data arranged in XYZ columns, or virtual matrix.

or

  • Image: An Image window. Multi-frame image is supported. Refer to this page below to see how to flip through all frames.

Creating the Graph

Activate a matrix or a worksheet or an image window.

From the menu, choose Plot > Contour: Contour Profiles

or

Click the Contour Profiles button on the 3D and Contour Graphs toolbar.

Template

profile_contour.otpu

(Installed to the Origin program folder).
