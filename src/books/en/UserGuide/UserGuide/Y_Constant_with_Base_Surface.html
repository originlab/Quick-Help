29.16.3 Y Constant with Base Surface

Y-Const-Base-Surface

Contents

  • 1 Data Requirements
  • 2 Creating the Graph
  • 3 Template
  • 4 Notes

Data Requirements

  • Worksheet: A block of worksheet cells (virtual matrix).

or

  • Matrix: A matrix of Z values.

Creating the Graph

Activate the matrixsheet or select required data from worksheet.

From the menu, choosev Plot> 3D : 3D Y Constant with Base.

or

Click the 3D Y Constant with Base button on the 3D and Contour Graphs toolbar.

Template

  • glYCONST.OTP (OpenGL)
  • YCONST.OTP

(Installed to the Origin program folder).

Notes

The Z values determine a surface of grid lines parallel to the X axis. The front surface displays a fill color. X and Y side walls are also enabled.
