4.8.6 The Remove Links Dialog Box

RemoveLinks-Dialog

One can insert links or formula in Origin worksheet for recalculation or auto-update. Those who do not use these tools don't need to concern themselves with disabling the feature. However, those who do use these tools may find that it suits their workflow to disable the linked cells/columns and that is the purpose of this dialog.


To open this tool:

  • Select Edit: Remove Links....

This dialog is used to replace formula, links with their current values.

Remove Links in Select to remove formula and links in the scope of
  • Project
  • Active Book
  • Active Sheet
  • Current Selection
Cell Formula and Links Check this to replace cell formula and links with their current values.
