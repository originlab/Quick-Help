15.3.2.7 Fit Curve Tab (Lower Panel)

NLFit-Dialog-FitCurveTab

The raw data and the fitted curve are plotted on the Fit Curve tab. This tab is only active when the Show Preview on Source Graph check box of Fitted Curves (Settings tab) is selected. Three buttons: have been provided in the graph for zoom in, zoom out, and rescale the curve, repsectively.
