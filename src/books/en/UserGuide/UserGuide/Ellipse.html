Brief Description

Implicit ellipse equation whose major and minor axes coincide with XY axes.

Sample Curve

Parameters

Number: 4

Names: xc, yc, a, b

Meanings: xc = x center, yc = y center, a = major radius, b = minor radius

Lower Bounds: a > 0.0, b > 0.0

Upper Bounds: none
