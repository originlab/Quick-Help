4.4.9 Stacking and Unstacking Worksheet Columns

Wks-Stacking-Unstacking-Cols

Stacking and unstacking worksheet columns provide methods to rearrange grouped data for statistics or other needs. Both stacking and unstacking allow including non-stacked or non-unstacked columns, and provide option for whether to consider the missing values. Besides, stacking columns is capable of stacking by rows, dividing stacked columns into subgroups.
