27.6 Customizing Toolbars

Customize-Toolbars

Toolbar and dockable window configurations are saved upon exiting Origin. You can customize toolbar configurations in a variety of ways:

  • Show or hide toolbars.
  • Add or remove toolbar buttons.
  • Program a button in the User Defined button group to perform a custom task.
  • Create your own bitmaps, use them to build toolbar buttons, then program the toolbar buttons to perform your custom tasks.
  • You can modify custom button groups (toolbars).
