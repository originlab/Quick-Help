FAQ-1088 Why are there duplicated points on my box chart?

Duplicated-Points-BoxChart

Last Update: 3/14/2021

Sometimes when you plot a Box chart with data points shown, you may find dublicated data points. It is caused by that both data points and outliers habe been turned on. it will thus make those outlier points be plotted twice.

To remove the duplicated data points:

  1. Double click on the box to open Plot Details dailog.
  2. Go to Box tab. Turn off Outliers..
