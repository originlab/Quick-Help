FAQ-881 How to add an Arrow at the Begin/End of the axis?

Add_Arrow_At_Axis

Last Update: 9/12/2017


On the graph, you could add an Arrow at the Begin/End of the axis in Axis dialog:

  1. Double click on the axis to open the Axis dialog.
  2. Go to Line and Ticks tab, then expand Arrow node under Line branch.
  3. Select the Arrow at Begin or Arrow at End check box.
  4. Also, you can expand the node of Arrow at Begin or Arrow at End to specific the format of the Arrow.

Keywords:axis, arrow, arrow at begin, arrow at end

Minimum Origin Version Required: 2018 SR0
