1.213 FAQ-956 Why my graphs in WPS can not be opened by double-clicking?

Fail-to-open-Graphs-in-WPS

Last Update: 6/22/2018

Origin graphs don't support been embedded into a WPS Office instance as an OLE(Object Linking and Embedding) object, for the present.

You can switch to Microsoft Office applications(such as MS Word, MS PowerPoint) to make Origin act as an OLE server.

Keywords:OLE, WPS Office, graph object in WPS, editable graph object
