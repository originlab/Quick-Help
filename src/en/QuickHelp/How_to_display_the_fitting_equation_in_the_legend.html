3.104 FAQ-635 How to display the fitting equation in the legend?

Display-Fitting-Equation

Last Update: 8/8/2018

The fitting equation formula is saved in Notes node in Nonlinear Curve Fit's Report Sheet.

To display the fitting equation in the graph legend,

  1. Copy the Equation in the Report Sheet.
  2. Paste it in the graph legend.

Note: You can also click Text tool (i.e. "T") button in the Tools toolbar. Click on the location in the graph where you want to put the equation. And then paste the equation inside the text object.
