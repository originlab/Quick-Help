1.42 FAQ-158 How do I hide legends of the hidden plots?

Last Update: 11/13/2018

Hide-hidden-plot-legend

Since Origin 2015 SR0, when you hide a data plot the related plot legend will be automatically hidden. The control of this option is the item Show Legend for Visible Plots Only in the context menu of Legend when you right click on the legend or go to menu Graph: Legend: Update Legend to open Update Data Plots Legend: legendupdate dialog.

Keywords:legend, plot hiding

Minimum Origin Version Required: 2015 SR0
