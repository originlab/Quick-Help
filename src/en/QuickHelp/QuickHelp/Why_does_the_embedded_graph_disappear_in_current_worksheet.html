2.111 FAQ-1025 Why does the embedded graph disappear or show partially in current worksheet?

Embedded-Graphs-disappeared

Last Update: 11/7/2019

Basically, you can zoom out or pan the worksheet to show the floating graphs embedded in the rightmost gray area of the worksheet.

If the embedded graph still doesn't show or just show in partial, you can select View: Arrange Graphs menu to get the graph back to the view. Please note, if there are no floating graphs, this menu will be disable.

Keywords:floating graph, embedded into worksheet, part of graph, zoom out, pan
