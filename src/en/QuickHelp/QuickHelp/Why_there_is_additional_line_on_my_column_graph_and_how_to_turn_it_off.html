1.246 FAQ-1035 Why there is additional line on my column graph? How can I turn it off?

Last Update: 9/28/2020

Turnoff-Additional-Line-Column

Column chart grows up from Y = 0 by default. If you rescale the Y axis range not to start from 0, you will see an additional line at Y = 0. To turn off this line,

  1. Double click on the Y axis to open the Axis dialog.
  2. Activate Grid tab. Uncheck "Y=" under Additional Lines.

Tips: if you want your column/bar plot starts from the axis line rather than additional line, please refer to this page.
