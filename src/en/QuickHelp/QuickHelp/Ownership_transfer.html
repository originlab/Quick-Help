6.51 FAQ-790 Can I transfer my Origin license to another person? How do I do this?

Ownership-Transfer

Last Update: 10/19/2015

If you are looking to transfer the ownership of an Origin or OriginPro license to another user, please send an email to reginfo@originlab.com. Please include the contact information for the purchasing agent at your University, Business, or Government Agency. You will also need to provide us with the contact information for the user that you want to transfer the license to.

Contact Information needed:

  • Name
  • Email
  • Phone
  • Mailing Address
  • OriginLab Web Membership User ID.
