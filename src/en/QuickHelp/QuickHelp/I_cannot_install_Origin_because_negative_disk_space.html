6.72 FAQ-1028 When I try to install Origin, I see a negative amount of disk space available and cannot install. How can I install Origin?

Insert-Negative-amount-of-Disk-Space

Last Update: 11/20/2019

This problem does not occur in Origin 9.1 or higher version. However, in Origin 9.0 SR0 and SR1, if your hard drive available space is higher than 2TB, you will encounter this issue. In this case, please contact OriginLab to obtain the Origin 9.0 SR2 installer.
