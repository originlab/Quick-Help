3.68 FAQ-305 How do I compute autocorrelation on a signal?

AutoCorrelate-Signal

Last Update: 2/4/2015

Autocorrelation or self correlation is simply the correlation of the signal with itself. From the main menu choose Analysis: SignalProcessing: Correlation and for Input1 and Input2 choose the same dataset.
