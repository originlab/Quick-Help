5.18 FAQ-357 Why does my graph look different on other computers?

Graph-Diff-on-other-PC

Last Update: 2/4/2015

You may need to update the graph to the current Windows default printer. To do so, right-click in the gray area of the plot (outside the printable part of the page) and select Fit Page to Printer from the context menu.
