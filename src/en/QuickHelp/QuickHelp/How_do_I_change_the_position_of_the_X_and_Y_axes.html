1.5 FAQ-115 How do I change the position of the X and Y axes?

Change-XY-Axis-Position

Last Update: 1/2/2018

2D

  1. To roughly move axis to new position, simply drag the axis.
  2. To precisely move axis to a position, double-click on the axis to open the Axis dialog. Go to the Line and Ticks tab, set the Axis Position.

3D

  1. Click once in graph to select the graph frame.
  2. Double click to open Plot Details dialog for layer.
  3. On Position tab, change Position from Auto to At Position and enter exact value.

Note: As long as position isn't Auto, user can later select the plane and drag it to new position.
