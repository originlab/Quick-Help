1.209 FAQ-952 How to keep line trend around the axis break?

Keep-line-trend-around-break

Last Update: 6/28/2018

When there is a break on the axis, you may want to keep the actual line trend(line slope) around the break.

To keep the line trend around the break,

  1. Double-click on the gray space outside the graph layer to open the Plot Details dialog.
  2. With the graph level selected in the left panel, go to the Display tab in the right panel. Check the Keep Line Trend as if no Axis Break check box.

Keywords:not break line at break, keep line trend, keep slope, connect axis breaks
