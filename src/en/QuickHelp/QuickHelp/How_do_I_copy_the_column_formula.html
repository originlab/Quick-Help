2.47 FAQ-483 How do I copy the column formula?

Copy-with-formula

Last Update: 10/2/2015

Highlight the column with a column formula defined in the Set Values dialog (indicated by a formula in the F(x)= column label row), right click and choose Copy:Copy(Formula) (or press Ctrl+J). Then you can paste the same column formula to another column.

To quickly toggle the display of the F(x)= column label row, use the hot key Ctrl+Shift+U.

Keywords:column formula, copy, paste

Minimum Origin Version Required: 9.1 SR0
