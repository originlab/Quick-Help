2.51 FAQ-489 How can I easily identify a particular worksheet within a workbook that contains many?

Identify-Wks-in-Multisheets-Wkb

Last Update: 6/28/2021

When the workbook contains many worksheets, you can use the Navigate Worksheets dialog to identify and navigate to a particular sheet.


To access this dialog:

  1. Right-click on the sheet name (sheet tab) and click Navigate ....
