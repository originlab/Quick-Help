2.39 FAQ-475 How do I resize width/height of multiple columns/rows in a worksheet?

Resize-MultiColRow-WH

Last Update: 10/2/2015

If you select more than one whole column or one whole row, even non-contiguous selections, you can manually resize all highlighted columns/rows simultaneously.

To manually resize, point to the space at the right edge of a column selection or the bottom edge of a row selection, and when the pointer becomes a double-headed arrow, drag the edge of the column/row heading.

This can be very useful in Analysis Report Sheets. In any table, right-click and choose View: Column Header. Now you can select multiple columns to manually resize.
