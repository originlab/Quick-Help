3.81 FAQ-319 How can I perform t-tests on rows?

t-tests-on-rows

Last Update: 2/4/2015


Origin supports paired-sample t-test and two-sample t-test on rows. These tools are accessible from the Statistics: Hypothesis Testing menu:

  • Two-Sample t-test on Rows
  • Pair-Sample t-test on Rows



Keywords:t-tests on row, row t-tests, two-sample t-test, pair-sample t-test, t-test

Minimum Origin Version Required: 2015 SR0
