1.250 FAQ-1041 Why does the embedded graphs fail to show in my Word file?

Embedded-Graphs-Fail-to-Show

Last Update: 2/11/2020

Sometimes, when you open a Word file with embedded Origin graphs, the embedded graphs might fail to show and just display the placeholders {EMBED Origin50.Graph}. In this case, you can simply press ALT+F9 hotkey to solve this. For more detailed information, please refer to this blog from Word support.
