6.87 FAQ-1111 I purchased Maintenance, but still getting message that my Maintenance expired when I open Origin. How can I update the Maintenance Information?

Update-License-After-Renew-Maintenance

Last Update: 12/12/2021

After purchased/renewed your Maintenance service, you will need to update your license to show the new Maintenance information.

If you have a node-locked license

  1. Launch Origin. Go to Help: Activate License menu.
  2. In the dialog, press Activate button to get the new license, which includes the up-to-date Maintenance information.

You can confirm the change by checking Help: About Origin menu and seeing the correct date
