1.228 FAQ-994 How to order column or bars in column or bar plot?

Last Update: 2/7/2019

Change-Column-Bar-Order

Though user can't drag to move orders of column/bars directly in the graph, by setting the X column as Categorical and axis tick labels as Categorical, user can change category orders in worksheet. The order of column/bars in graph will update automatically.

Keywords:sort, order, categorical, bar

Minimum Origin Version Required: 2015
