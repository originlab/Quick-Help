5.74 FAQ-1030 How do I turn Mini Toolbars off or on?

turn-mini-toolbars-on-off

Last Update: 5/31/2022

Origin 2020 introduced Mini Toolbars for fast-editing of graph properties. When the user clicks on a graph object -- be it the page, the graph layer, graph axes, or a plot or other graphic object -- a popup toolbar of object-specific tools appears next to the click point.



You can turn Mini Toolbars off by pressing Ctrl+SHIFT+T or by unchecking Mini Toolbars under View menu.

You can turn it back on by pressing the keys again or check the menu again.
