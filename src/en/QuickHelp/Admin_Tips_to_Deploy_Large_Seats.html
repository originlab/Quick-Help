6.64 FAQ-902 I am the Administrator of a Lab. I need install Origin on 100 computers. What is the best way?

Admin-Tips-to-Deploy-Large-Seats

Last Update: 5/16/2019

As a benefit of maintenance service, you can request a group product key for your serial number. Install Origin with this group product key. Origin will activate with the proper real Product key after it runs for the first time.

Refer to the second tip in this page for details of how to request and use a group product key.
