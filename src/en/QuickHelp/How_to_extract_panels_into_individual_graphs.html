1.211 FAQ-945 How to extract panels into individual graphs?

Extract-Graphs

Last Update: 6/27/2018

To extract panels of a multi-layers graph into individual graphs,

  • With the graph window activated, select Graph: Extract Graphs menu to open the Extract to Graphs dialog.

In this dialog, you can select the desired layers to extract. To Extract all layers, enter 1:0 in the Extracted Layers text box; If you want to extract specified layers, just enter the layer index with comma as separator.

You can also specify whether to keep the original layer size or full page for the extracted graphs.
