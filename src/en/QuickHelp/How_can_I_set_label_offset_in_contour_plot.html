1.64 FAQ-186 How can I move labels in my contour plot?

contour-label-offset

Last Update: 4/26/2022

To reposition contour labels, click on the label and drag it with your mouse along the same contour line.


For more on labeling contour plots, see FAQ-925 Why do the contour line labels disappear from my contour plot?.
